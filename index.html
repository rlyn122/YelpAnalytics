How does cuisine impact language used for reviews?

A cultural analysis by Ryan Lin and Noah Lian

Setup: Hypothesis and Goals

Our research will investigate how cuisine influences the type of adjectives and words that consumers use across four different cuisines: Chinese, American, Mexican, and Japanese to write reviews using the Yelp Open Dataset.

Code to this presentation and results can be found on Github.
